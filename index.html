About AS Cricket Bats

AS Sports is a renowned cricket equipment manufacturer based in Pakistan, known for crafting high-quality cricket bats and gear since 1965. With decades of craftsmanship, innovation, and a commitment to excellence, AS has earned a solid reputation among domestic and international cricketers alike. Our bats are made using Grade A English Willow and Premium Kashmir Willow, offering players the perfect combination of durability and striking power.Each AS cricket bat goes through rigorous quality checks and hand-finishing processes to ensure maximum performance and longevity.
